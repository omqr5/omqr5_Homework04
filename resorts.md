# Top Snowboard Resorts

These resorts are known for quality terrain, good conditions, and fun for all skill levels.

## 1. Myrkdalen (Norway)
- Close to Bergen  
- Lots of snowfall  
- Beginner-friendly + off-piste options  

## 2. Whistler Blackcomb (Canada)
- Huge terrain  
- Great parks  
- Long season  

## 3. Niseko United (Japan)
- Deepest powder in the world  
- Great food + culture  

## 4. Breckenridge (USA)
- Iconic terrain parks  
- Reliable snow
