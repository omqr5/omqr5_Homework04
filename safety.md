# Snowboarding Safety & Avalanche Basics

Riding all season means staying safe on and off the mountain.

## General Safety
- Wear a helmet 
- Warm up before runs (stretch) 
- Follow signs and resort rules  
- Watch out for ice patches and crowded areas  

## Avalanche Basics
If you ever ride off-trail:
- Know the avalanche forecast  
- Bring a beacon, shovel, and probe  
- Never go alone  
- Avoid slopes between 30–45° in unstable snow
