# Snowboarding Gear Guide

Snowboarding requires the right equipment to stay safe, warm, and have fun.
Below is a breakdown of important gear and what to look for.

## Essential Gear

### 1. Board Types
- All-Mountain (best for beginners and general riding)
- Freestyle (flexible, great for tricks)
- Freeride (stiffer, made for powder)

### 2. Bindings
Two Main styles; 
- Strap-in (most common)
- Step-on (quick entry)

### 3. Boots
- Flex rating  
- Lacing system (boa, speed lace, traditional)

### 4. Clothing Layers
- Base Layer (Wicks sweat, wool for cold weather)
- Mid Layer (Isulates Warmth)
- Shell/Jacket (Wind & waterproof protection)
