# Best Countries for Snowboarding

Snowboarding around the world offers unique terrain, scenery, and snow conditions.
Here are some of the top places to ride.

## 1. Norway
- Legendary fjord views  
- Deep winter snowpack  
- Great for beginners + experts  
- Top resorts: Trysil, Hemsedal, Myrkdalen  

## 2. Japan
- World-class powder ("Japow")  
- Consistent snowfall  
- Best for tree runs  
- Top resorts: Niseko, Hakuba, Rusutsu  

## 3. Switzerland
- Expensive but elite  
- Massive alpine terrain  
- Famous resorts like Zermatt & St. Moritz  

## 4. USA
- Huge variety of parks, powder, and mountains  
- Best regions: Colorado, Utah, California
